How We Started

Tap to expand

Capturing moments, creating memories. Welcome to Memories Photography Studio – A Self-Shoot Experience!

We are May and Kelvin, both CPA accountants, but more than that — parents who love taking photos of our children and every milestone in their lives. There were times, however, when we couldn’t capture those moments the way we wanted because the studios in Indang and nearby areas were just too expensive. We always felt sayang for the memories we missed.

One day, May asked a simple question that changed everything: “Bakit hindi na lang tayo magpatayo ng sarili nating studio?” At first, we built it just for us — so our family would never have to miss those moments again. But soon we realized: what about others who feel the same way we did?

That’s when we decided to open Memories Photography Studio to everyone. A self-shoot studio made with love, designed to be affordable, fun, and accessible — so no family has to miss out on capturing the memories that truly matter.

📸 Here, every click is a story, every photo a memory — for you, for us, for everyone.

🌱 What Our New Logo Means

Our new logo for Memories Photography Studio is more than just a fresh design—it’s a reflection of our values and our love for community and sustainability.

  • The Circular Frame
    Symbolizes the unbroken cycle of memories and community—how every moment we capture continues to inspire and connect us all.
  • The Leaf Motif
    Stands for growth, sustainability, and our promise to care for the environment. It represents our eco-friendly choices, from reusable props to LED lighting and digital-first services.
  • The Heart Icon
    Nestled within our logo, the heart represents the love and passion behind every photo we take—whether it’s for your family, your milestones, or for giving back to the community. It’s a reminder that every session is filled with care, empathy, and joy shared with others.
  • Warm, Earthy Colors
    Inspired by nature, our new palette reflects our Filipino roots and our commitment to stay grounded, accessible, and eco-conscious.
  • Handwritten Script
    This adds warmth and a personal touch, making every interaction with our studio feel special and genuine.
  • Modern Simplicity
    Clean lines and a minimalist look reinforce our mission of making high-quality, affordable photography available for everyone.
