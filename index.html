Our Story

At Memories Photography Studio, we believe every moment deserves to be captured. Our journey began with a love for photographing our kids, celebrating their milestones, and cherishing life’s special moments. We noticed that self-shoot studios in our area were not affordable for many, so we decided to create our own. With a passion for photography and a camera at home, we set out to offer an accessible and affordable self-shoot experience. Our goal is simple: to make photography fun and available to everyone who, like us, loves taking photos but is sometimes held back by cost. Whether it’s a special occasion or just a moment you want to remember, we’re here to help you create beautiful memories.
